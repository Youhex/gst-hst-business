Questions about filing or your account

If you have questions about filing your return or issues specific to your account, please contact us by phone.

Feedback about CRA's online filing services

If you have suggestions about how we can improve NETFILE or GST/HST Internet File Transfer, or if you have feedback about filing GST/HST returns through your online CRA account, please send us your thoughts.

To share your feedback, please email us at: netcom@cra-arc.gc.ca.

If you do email us, please do not share personal or confidential information such as your business information or your access code. These emails are not encrypted.

Do not expect a reply. In particular, we will not respond to questions about your own or your business's income tax situation. For questions about taxes or to make changes to your account, please contact us by phone.

Liaison officer support for small businesses

Certain small and medium-sized businesses can get help understanding their tax obligations through our Liaison Officer service. This is a voluntary program. If you participate, a liaison officer will help you:

  • Understand your tax obligations
  • Find out where you can get more information
  • Learn the common mistakes businesses make
  • Get answers to your questions

Refer to: Liaison Officer service
