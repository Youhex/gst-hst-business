Hours of service - electronic methods

CRA electronic filing is available seven days a week, during the following hours:

  • Pacific time: 3:00 am to 11:30 pm
  • Mountain time: 4:00 am to 12:30 am
  • Central time: 5:00 am to 1:30 am
  • Eastern time: 6:00 am to 2:30 am
  • Atlantic time: 7:00 am to 3:30 am
  • Newfoundland time: 7:30 am to 4:00 am

    • Browser requirements

    To make sure you can file your return securely and confidentially, you must use a Web browser that supports the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol. If you don't have a browser that meets the security standard, you can either download a complete browser package or upgrade the one you have. Upgrade to TLS 1.2 so that your system is as secure as possible.

    If your browser does not meet the requirements listed above, you may not be able to use GST/HST NETFILE or GST/HST Internet File Transfer.

    The Canada Revenue Agency is not responsible for any difficulties or problems in downloading and installing software. Technical support is provided by the software suppliers.

    Browser settings

    You must ensure that cookies and JavaScript are enabled in your browser settings.

    Since not all browsers are configured in the same manner, you should check the Internet options of your browser for ways to enable the cookies and JavaScript functions.
