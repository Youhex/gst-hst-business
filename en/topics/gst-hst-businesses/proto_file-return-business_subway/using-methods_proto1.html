Hours of service and browser requirements for electronic filing

If you are using a CRA method to file electronically (through your CRA account, the online NETFILE form, TELEFILE, or your accounting software), the service is available 7 days a week.

The service is available for the following hours each day:

Pacific time:
3:00 am to 11:30 pm
Mountain time:
4:00 am to 12:30 am
Central time:
5:00 am to 1:30 am
Eastern time:
6:00 am to 2:30 am
Atlantic time:
7:00 am to 3:30 am
Newfoundland time:
7:30 am to 4:00 am

Browser requirements

There are certain minimum security standards your browser must meet for you to be able to use CRA online filing methods.

Confirm your browser settings meet the requirements
